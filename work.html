LLoyds Bank

Visual design for an app with 4m+ users

  • CLIENT: LLOYDS BANK
  • PROJECT:MOBILE 3.0

I was hired by LLoyds to help replatform their existing mobile banking application and work on design and prototypes for the next version. I created style guides and a pattern library to bring cohesion to the app and design process, as well as new ways of working.

I also took part in a number of hack days where I created prototypes for a Google Wear android app and banking app enhancements for customers with mental health issues.
